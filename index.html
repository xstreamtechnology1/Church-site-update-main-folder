Our Beliefs

The Bible

The inspired Word of God and is the final authority in all matters concerning Christian doctrine, conduct and work (II Tim. 3:15-17; Prov.3:5; Rev. 22:18, 19).

The Godhead

Consists of three separate, distinct, and recognizable personalities and qualities, perfectly united in one (I Jn. 5:7; Mt. 3:16, 17; I Cor. 13:14; Mt. 28:19).

Jesus Christ

JESUS is the ONLY BEGOTTEN SON of GOD, He was born of a virgin, was crucified, died, was buried and He resurrected bodily. He was not created but rather created all things (Jn. 1:1-3; 14:18; Is. 7:14; Mt. 1:18-25; Rom. 1:4; I Cor. 15:3,4).

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit to transform lives.

Repentance

We believe in true repentance as a necessary step towards salvation.

Marriage

Marriage is binding for life and must be between two born-again Christians (Gen. 2:24; Mt. 19:3‚Äì9; Rom. 7:2, 3).

Rapture

Rapture is the catching away of all Christian saints (Dead or alive) from the earth (I Thess. 4:16-18; Jn. 14:1-3).
